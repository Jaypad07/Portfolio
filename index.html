About me

Hi, welcome to my portfolio! My name is Jay, and I'm fueled by a deep passion for technology and personal growth. My professional journey has been diverse, starting with rewarding experiences as a Longshoreman and working with United Airlines. However, I soon realized that I wanted more challenges and personal development. That's why I made the decision to return to school and pursue a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

Alongside my formal education, I've taken the initiative to self-study programming through online platforms like Freecodecamp.org and Codegym.cc. This has equipped me with practical skills in HTML, CSS, and Java. As a lifelong learner, I am always seeking new opportunities to enhance my skills and contribute to collaborative teams.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I find joy in various hobbies such as dancing, spending time with friends, and immersing myself in video games and books. What sets me apart is my unwavering commitment to personal and professional growth, paired with my strong work ethic. I thrive on challenges and embrace them with determination, always striving to contribute to the success of a team. With my dedication and ability to collaborate effectively, I aim to make a positive impact in any endeavor.

Projects

As a beginner project in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I took on the challenge of creating a fun two player game from scratch. I created a 3x3 grid with a Stranger Things backdrop. The game incorporates essential web development concepts like Grid CSS and Flexbox for effective element organization. With player turns indicated through a custom-designed walkie-talkie interface, you can immerse yourself in the world of Stranger Things.
A Rock, Paper, Scissors game developed in Java, playable in the console. Featuring both Player vs. Player and Player vs. Computer modes. Challenge your friends or face off against an AI villain as you choose your moves while keeping track of your win/loss records. Planned using GitHub projects, this project allowed me to sharpen my Java skills and deliver a fun gaming experience.
